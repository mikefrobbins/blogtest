Robbins is a former Microsoft MVP, creator of <em>The PowerShell Conference Book</em>, author of <em>PowerShell 101: The No-Nonsense Beginner’s Guide to PowerShell</em>, co-author of <em>Windows PowerShell TFM 4th Edition</em>, and a contributing author in the <em>PowerShell Deep Dives</em> book. Mike has been a strong supporter of the PowerShell community and is now the lead writer for <a href="https://docs.microsoft.com/en-us/powershell/azure">Azure PowerShell</a> at Microsoft. He blogs at <a href="https://mikefrobbins.com/">mikefrobbins.com</a> and can be found on twitter <a href="https://twitter.com/mikefrobbins">@mikefrobbins</a>.

All content written on this site before March 23th, 2020 was created prior to Mike joining Microsoft.
